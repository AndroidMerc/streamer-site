# Official Contest Rules: AndroidMerc's 7th Year Launch Party Giveaway
1. **Eligibility**
	- This contest is open to legal residents of **Canada, the United States, the United Kingdom, and Australia.**
	- Participants must be at least **18 years of age** or the age of majority in their jurisdiction.
	- **Ineligibility:** The Sponsor (AndroidMerc) and any person residing in the same household as the Sponsor are ineligible. Immediate family members (parents, siblings, spouse, children) are ineligible. Extended family (nieces, nephews, cousins) and Channel Moderators are eligible to win.
	- **Void where prohibited by law.**
2. **No Purchase Necessary & Methods of Entry**
	- No purchase, payment, or "Bits/Subscription" is required to enter or win.
	- Paid support does not increase chances of winning.
	- **Method A (Random Draws):** Viewers may enter periodic random draws by **typing !prizetoken** in the Twitch chat during designated entry windows. Entry windows will remain open for at least 60 seconds following any commercial breaks to ensure all viewers have an equal opportunity to enter.
	- **Method B (Community Games):** Viewers may enter by **participating in designated community games** (e.g., Jackbox, Marbles on Stream, etc.) during the stream. Winners of these games will be eligible for prizes subject to the "Skill-Testing Question" requirement in Section 6. Participation in games is on a first-come, first-served basis due to lobby limits.
3. **Prize Distribution & Limits**
	- **Limit:** One (1) prize per person for the duration of the stream.
	- **Ineligible Winners:** If an ineligible person (e.g., the Sponsor or a previous winner) wins a game or draw, the prize will be awarded to the **next highest-ranking eligible participant** (e.g., 2nd place) or a new random entrant will be drawn.
	- **Prize Value:** Total prize pool is approximately **$100.00 CAD.** Individual prizes range from $2.49 to $25.00 CAD in value.
4. **International Prize Substitution**
	- For winners outside of Canada, or where shipping costs exceed reasonable limits, the Sponsor reserves the right to substitute a physical prize with a **digital gift card of equal value** (e.g., a Steam Wallet Code).
5. **Shipping, Taxes, and VAT**
	- The Sponsor will cover standard shipping costs.
	- **Winner Liability:** International winners are **solely responsible** for any local customs duties, taxes, or Value Added Tax (VAT) required by their country upon delivery.
6. **The Skill-Testing Question (Requirement)**
	- To comply with the **Canadian Criminal Code** and international "Game of Skill" standards, every potential winner must correctly answer a multi-step mathematical skill-testing question (e.g., [7 &times; 10] + 14 - 7) without assistance.
	- The test is administered via **Twitch Whisper.** The entrant has **60 seconds** to reply with the correct answer to be declared the official winner.
7. **Winner Notification & Data Collection**
	- Winners will be announced live by the bot. To officially claim a prize, the selected entrant must:
		1. Type **!claim** in the Twitch chat within sixty (60) seconds of the announcement to stay eligible.
		2. Correctly answer the skill-testing question via Whisper.
		3. **Provide their Legal Name, Email Address, and Country of Residence.**
	- If the selected entrant fails to claim (by typing !claim) within the sixty (60) second window, they will be disqualified and a new winner will be drawn.
	- Winners must be able to receive Twitch Whispers from the Sponsor. **To ensure your privacy settings do not block the notification, it is highly recommended that participants follow the channel or temporarily disable "Block Whispers from Strangers" in their Security & Privacy settings.**
	- Prizes distributed through the merch store will also require the winner's shipping address at the time of redemption on the store's platform.
	- Failure to provide required info or pass the skill test within the time limit results in forfeiture.
 	- A list of winners (Twitch Username and Prize won) will be available upon request via Twitch Whisper for 30 days following the event.
9. **Privacy Statement**
	- Personal information is collected strictly for prize delivery and legal record-keeping per **PIPEDA** and the **Competition Act.**
	- We will not sell your data or use it for marketing. Data will be stored securely and deleted 12 months after the event.
	- By entering, you consent to your Twitch username being announced live and potentially displayed in a "Winners Circle" on the channel.
10. **Limitation of Liability**
	- By participating, you release AndroidMerc and Twitch Interactive, Inc. from any liability or damage.
